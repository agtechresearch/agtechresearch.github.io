Introduction

Welcome to the AgTech Lab

Mission \& Vision

The AgTech Research Laboratory is dedicated to pioneering the future of agriculture by integrating advanced information technology (IT) with agricultural science. Our mission is to significantly enhance agricultural productivity, efficiency, and sustainability. Through rigorous research in data-driven precision agriculture, robotics, computer vision, and intelligent environmental control, we aim to establish a new paradigm for global food production and contribute to the sustainable development of humanity.

Research Vision

Innovative Research

Development of innovative agricultural systems using cutting-edge technology

Sustainable Agriculture

Realization of environmentally friendly and sustainable agriculture

Talent Development

Training experts to lead future agriculture

Main Research Areas

Smart Agricultural Robots

Agricultural automation and precision farming using robotics and artificial intelligence

  • Autonomous agricultural robot development
  • Automated harvesting and transportation systems
  • Agricultural work efficiency and labor reduction

Computer Vision

Crop growth and environment monitoring based on image processing and deep learning

  • Crop status and growth analysis
  • Disease and pest detection and prediction
  • Real-time environmental data collection

Environmental Control Systems

Development of intelligent environmental control and optimization systems

  • Greenhouse/smart farm environment automatic control
  • Data-based optimization algorithms
  • Energy efficiency and sustainability improvement
