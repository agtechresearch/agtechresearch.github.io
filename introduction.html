Introduction

Welcome to the AgTech Laboratory

Laboratory Introduction

The AgTech Laboratory aims to enhance agricultural productivity, efficiency, and sustainability through the convergence of advanced information technology (IT) and agricultural technology (AgTech).
We research innovative technologies in various fields including precision agriculture, automation, computer vision, robotics, and environmental control.
By utilizing data-driven decision making, artificial intelligence (AI), and robotics, we present new paradigms for future agriculture.

Our main research topics include smart agricultural robots, computer vision-based crop/environment monitoring, and intelligent environmental control systems.
Our laboratory contributes to sustainable agriculture, environmental protection, and solving practical problems in agricultural fields.

Main Research Areas

Smart Agricultural Robots

Agricultural automation and precision farming using robotics and artificial intelligence

  • Autonomous agricultural robot development
  • Automated harvesting and transportation systems
  • Agricultural work efficiency and labor reduction

Computer Vision

Crop growth and environment monitoring based on image processing and deep learning

  • Crop status and growth analysis
  • Disease and pest detection and prediction
  • Real-time environmental data collection

Environmental Control Systems

Development of intelligent environmental control and optimization systems

  • Greenhouse/smart farm environment automatic control
  • Data-based optimization algorithms
  • Energy efficiency and sustainability improvement
