Research Projects

Development and Demonstration of an Integrated Intelligent Vertical Farming Systems with Protected Fruit Vegetables

Funding: IPET / KoSFarm
Period: 2024.04 - Ongoing

The research focuses on the development and demonstration of an intelligent vertical farming system for facility-grown crops. It aims to establish a crop growth monitoring system for individual plants and develop an AI model for optimal control by analyzing environmental and growth data. Additionally, the study integrates an automated harvesting system with monitoring and transportation technologies to enhance agricultural automation.

Development of an AI and Agricultural Robot-Driven Vertical Cultivation System for Korean Melons

Funding: GARES
Period: 2024.04 - Ongoing

This research project focuses on the development of an AI and robotics-based vertical cultivation system for oriental melon. This includes the establishment of a monitoring system for crop growth, the development of an AI-driven algorithm to determine fruit location and harvest readiness, and the implementation of robotic navigation with obstacle detection and optimal path planning. Furthermore, the study aims to integrate key robotic harvesting technologies into a unified system to improve efficiency.

Funding: MAFRA

Basic Conceptualization and Feasibility Study for the Development of the Saemangeum Global Food Hub.

Research on developing greenhouse simulators to estimate crop yields and energy consumption

Funding: EPIS
Period: 2023.09 - 2024.12

Greenhouse environment and crop growth simulator with energy consumption estimation.

Smart Farm Platform for high-quality and traceable yield. A multi-purpose DDS based on proximal and remote sensing. (ALARAD)

Funding: IITP
Period: 2021.07 - 2024.06

To optimize the decision making process through the proximal and remote monitoring of meteorological, soil, plant and fruit identifying the critical physiological variables for health, growth and vigor in order to adapt and refine the standard cultivation techniques maximizing fruit yield, quality and food security while minimizing environmental impact and other risks.

Development of autonomus environment control platform for greenhouses using artificial intelligence

Funding: IPET
Period: 2021.04 - 2024.12

To develop an AI-based fully autonomous integrated environmental control technology for operating a smart farming, a complex environmental control platform will be created that allows cultivators to implement their desired cultivation strategies by monitoring real-time growth and environmental data.
